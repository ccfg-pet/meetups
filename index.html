SRJC Petaluma Walk & Connect

A casual walking group for SRJC students, faculty, and friends to move, meet, and recharge—right here on campus.

👟 Next Walk: Thursday, September 12

Start time: 12:15 PM (right after morning classes)
Meeting spot: Outside the Mahoney Library entrance
Route: Campus loop + nearby trails (about 2 miles)
Pace: Chill and chatty—no pressure

Bring water, wear comfy shoes, and come as you are. Whether you’re looking for fresh air, new friends, or just a break from screens, we’ve got you.

🗓️ Why We Walk

We started this group to make movement more social and less stressful. It’s not a workout—it’s a reset. No sign-up, no gear, no judgment. Just show up and walk with us.

Open to all SRJC Petaluma students, staff, and community members. New faces always welcome.

📸 Past Walks

We’ve wandered the quad, looped the wetlands, and even walked-and-talked through finals week. Got photos or stories? Send them to srjcpetalumawalk@email.com and we’ll feature them here.
